Front-end Developer

I am a front-end developer, graduate of Internet Technology, based in Minsk, Belarus. I am new in freelance, but I have 6+ years experience creating responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I write code that conforms to the W3C's web standards and semantic best practices. I use technologies like HTML5 (Jade), CSS3 (SASS) and JavaScript (jQuery), to transform static artwork into mobile first, pixel prefect, interactive and fully functional interfaces; exactly the way your designers intend them to be.

WordPress Developer

In many projects, client’s require the ability to manage the content of their website. To do this, I integrate front-end templates into WordPress themes and offer custom functionality via PHP.

WordPress has become the simplest and quickest way for me to develop new, fully content managed websites. Sell your products online or set up a blog, either way, you'll have the ability to manage your content just the way you want to with only the parts you really need and nothing in your way.
